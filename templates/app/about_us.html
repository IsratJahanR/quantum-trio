About CareHub

Transforming beauty and wellness in Bangladesh with seamless, trusted solutions.

Our Mission

At CareHub, we’re dedicated to making beauty and wellness services effortless, reliable, and delightful for everyone in Bangladesh. We know the frustrations of long wait times, unreliable scheduling, and outdated booking systems. Our mission is to simplify the entire experience—connecting customers with trusted salons, spas, and wellness experts while empowering businesses with smart tools to thrive. From instant bookings to transparent reviews, we’re building a platform that fosters trust, convenience, and quality.

We envision a community where self-care is accessible to all, and service providers are celebrated for their craft. CareHub is more than an app—it’s a movement to elevate beauty and wellness standards, one appointment at a time.

What We Offer

Effortless Booking

Book your favorite services with real-time availability. Filter by location, price, or ratings, and choose your preferred stylist or expert.

Seamless Communication

Connect with providers via in-app chat for quick queries, and stay updated with real-time booking notifications.

Trusted Quality

Choose providers with confidence using ratings and reviews, and share feedback to help improve services.

Our Story

At CareHub, we’re passionate about revolutionizing the beauty and wellness landscape in Bangladesh by bringing salons and beauty parlors into the digital age. Our mission is to make self-care seamless, enjoyable, and accessible for everyone, eliminating the frustrations of long wait times, unreliable appointments, and outdated booking methods. Through our innovative online platform, customers can effortlessly book services at trusted salons and parlors with just a few clicks, saving time and ensuring a delightful experience every time.

We’re equally committed to empowering salon and parlor owners by providing them with the tools to thrive in the digital world. By creating their own profiles on CareHub, they can showcase their services, connect with new clients, and build a vibrant online presence that boosts visibility and growth. CareHub is more than a booking platform—it’s a dynamic community where self-care is celebrated, businesses flourish, and every appointment sparks joy. Together, we’re creating a brighter, more connected future for beauty and wellness in Bangladesh, one confident smile at a time.
