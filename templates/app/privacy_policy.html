Privacy Policy

Your trust matters to us. Learn how we protect your information.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At CareHub, we are dedicated to safeguarding the privacy of our users—both customers booking beauty and wellness services and salon/parlor owners managing their online profiles. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform to book appointments, create business profiles, or engage with our services across Bangladesh.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a seamless booking experience, we collect:

  • Customer Information: Name, email, phone number, and booking preferences to facilitate appointments and communication with salons.
  • Salon Owner Information: Business name, contact details, service offerings, and location to create and manage online profiles.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with CareHub, such as search history, reviews, and ratings, to improve our services.
  • Payment Details: Securely processed payment information (via trusted third-party providers) for bookings, never stored directly on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process and confirm bookings between customers and salons.
  • Enable salon owners to showcase their services and connect with new clients.
  • Personalize your experience with tailored recommendations and notifications.
  • Gather insights to enhance CareHub’s functionality and user satisfaction.
  • Communicate updates, promotions, or support responses with your consent.

3. Data Protection

Your security is our priority. We implement industry-standard encryption and secure protocols to protect your information. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel, and we never sell or share your personal details with third parties, except as required to process bookings (e.g., payment gateways) or comply with legal obligations.

4. Your Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Update or delete your profile information at any time.
  • Opt out of promotional emails or notifications.
  • Contact us at support@carehub.com to request data access or removal.

5. Cookies and Tracking

CareHub uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, such as remembering your preferences and analyzing site usage. You can manage cookie settings in your browser.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. Changes will be posted on this page, and we’ll notify users of significant updates.

For questions about your privacy, reach out to us at support@carehub.com. At CareHub, your trust is the foundation of our community, and we’re committed to keeping it strong.
